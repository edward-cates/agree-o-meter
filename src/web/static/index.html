Agree-O-Meter

Share your ideas. See how much you want to be told they're good.

How it works

You'll share 5 ideas — things you're thinking about doing, building, or changing. The more you actually care about the idea, the better your result will be.

An AI will respond to each idea with different levels of encouragement, from "great idea!" to "terrible idea." You pick the response you prefer.

At the end, you'll get a score from 0–10 showing how much you prefer encouragement vs. honest pushback.

🔒

Totally anonymous

✓ Your ideas are sent to AI to generate responses — we don't store any text you type.

✓ The only thing saved is your score (0–10). No text, no cookies, no accounts.

✓ No way to link your scores or answers back to you.
