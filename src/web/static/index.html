Agree-O-Meter

At what point do you choose comfort over truth?

How it works

5 rounds of escalating personal questions. You share something real, then pick between two responses: one that validates you and one that challenges you.

The stakes go up each round. Early questions are easy. Later ones ask you to be vulnerable. The more honestly you answer, the more accurate your score.

At the end, you get a score showing where you fall on the comfort-vs-truth spectrum.

🔒

✓ Your answers are sent to AI to generate responses — we store nothing you type.

✓ The only thing saved is your score (0-10). No text, no cookies, no accounts.

✓ No way to link your score or answers back to you.
